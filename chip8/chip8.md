## Keyboard map
    Chip-8:    QWERTY keyboard:

    1 2 3 C        1 2 3 4
    4 5 6 D        Q W E R
    7 8 9 E        A S D F
    A 0 B F        Z X C V

Pressing Enter resets the emulator.

All the games seem to have different controls, so you'll just have to try them and see what works.
